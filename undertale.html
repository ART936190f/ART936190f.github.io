About Undertale

Undertale is a role-playing video game created by American indie developer Toby Fox. The player controls a child who has fallen into the Underground, a vast, secluded region under the surface of the Earth, separated by a magic barrier. The player meets various monsters during the journey, and the core mechanic revolves around choosing whether to kill, flee, or befriend them. The game was released on September 15, 2015.

Game Details:

The game's unique combat system and compelling narrative, where player choices significantly impact the storyline, have garnered widespread critical acclaim and a dedicated fanbase.

Characters:

The world of Undertale is rich with memorable characters.
